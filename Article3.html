A Promise to Earth

By Lebron Subrado  |  February 2026

Promise is to make something of our life that is meant to happen. Not in a dramatic sense, but in the way that actually matters, showing up, pushing through the hard days, and continuing to grow even when progress feels invisible. The Earth has sustained life through every kind of hardship imaginable, and I figure the least I can do is meet that endurance with some of my own.

There's something humbling about standing on a planet this old and realizing how brief your window really is. That realization doesn't scare me, it motivates me. The Earth doesn't pause for anyone, and neither should I.

"I was given one shot at existing on this planet. I refuse to let that go to nothing."

In the end, this isn't about grand declarations. It's about quietly honoring the opportunity of being alive by actually doing something with it. Succeeding, growing, contributing, these feel like the most honest ways I know how to give something back to a world that never asked for thanks, but deserves it anyway.
